About ArkVision

Shaping the future of the maritime industry through innovation and integrity.

Our Story

Founded in Singapore, ArkVision grew from a small team of maritime engineers to an industry leader respected for delivering advanced manufacturing solutions. We started with a simple goal: to build high‑quality components that empower vessels to navigate safely and efficiently. Today, our products help clients across the globe chart new waters.

From our early days fabricating hardware for coastal craft to our current projects designing integrated automation systems for ocean‑going ships, ArkVision remains guided by the same principles of excellence, integrity and client partnership.

Our Mission

To deliver reliable maritime manufacturing solutions that enhance safety, efficiency and sustainability on the seas.

Our Vision

To be the trusted partner for vessel builders worldwide, known for pushing the boundaries of marine technology through creativity and collaboration.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We build trust through honest communication and responsible action.
  • Innovation: We challenge the status quo, continuously seeking better solutions.
  • Collaboration: We succeed together with our clients, partners and communities.
  • Excellence: We strive for quality in every detail, from design to delivery.

Our Journey

2010

ArkVision Founded

A small team of engineers comes together with a passion for maritime manufacturing and establishes ArkVision in Singapore.

2015

First International Client

ArkVision supplies custom navigation modules for an offshore service fleet, marking our first global partnership.

2018

Automation Platform Launched

Release of our flagship automation system, enabling vessels to integrate smart control and data monitoring throughout their operations.

2023

Global Expansion

We open offices in Europe and the Middle East, bringing local support to our growing international customer base.

Distributorship

Ark Vision is proud to be appointed as distributor and sales & service agent of established companies. We continue to seek opportunities to co-operate or represent Marine equipment companies in this region.
